Storage and shipment: Logistics

Vermillion keeps its cargo in grain port silos, which meet all technical requirements. The goods are insured throughout storage and at all stages of shipment. We load containers immediately from the port silos or from the oil crushing facility. The products can be supplied in a variety of methods, such as containers, railway, truck, and bulk vessels. First-rate independent surveyor firms inspect the products during transshipment or container loading.
